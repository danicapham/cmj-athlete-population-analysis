# Correlation Heatmap

The heatmap shows the degree of correlation between the variables given in the datasets (jump height, RSI, peak power, eccentric peak power, landing impulse). It supports my fidning that jump height, RSI, and peak power are strongly related while eccentric peak power and landing impulse have a weaker degree of correlation to jump height. This finding helps justify my multivariate model.
