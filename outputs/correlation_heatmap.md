## Correlation Heatmap

The heatmap shows the degree of correlation between the variables given in the datasets (jump height, RSI, peak power, eccentric peak power, landing impulse). As observed in the chart, jump height, RSI modified, and peak power are strongly correlated together while eccentric peak power and landing impulse have weaker degrees of correlation to jump height.
